Pickle Juice Toast

Description

For days when you don't have a lot of time this is a great recipe to fuel the body and get some electrolytes. Easily made with little ingredients and a great way to get rid of that old jar of pickle juice in the fridge!

Ingredients

  • bread
  • pickle juice

Steps

  1. Either toast your bread to your desired burntness, or just place your bread in a bowl
  2. Pour pickle juice in the bowl and serve!
  3. Bon Appetite!
