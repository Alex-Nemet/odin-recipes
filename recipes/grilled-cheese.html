Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Description

Another great quick recipe to use up your old bread laying around. Awesome snack full of protein and carbs to get you through the day. Hot tip: if you want to make sure your cheese melts, place a pot lid over the sandwich while it's browning to perfection in the pan.

Ingredients

  • Your choice of bread (I suggest white bread)
  • Butter
  • Cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Heat up your pan to medium on the stove
  2. Butter the outsides of your sandwich
  3. Grate your cheddar and place on the inside of the bread
  4. Place sandwich in the pan and heat one side until browned
  5. Flip the sandwich and brown the other side
  6. Enjoy!
